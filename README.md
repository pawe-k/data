# data
How can we drive more views, impressions, and conversions organically, without spending a dime on ads? This is the pressing question of our time.
As more people turn to social media for direct-to-consumer marketing and as many accept that organic growth is nearly impossible, big tech continues to charge premium prices for advertising.
There’s no doubt that paid ads are effective and help businesses reach their ideal customers.
But what if our advertising budget was zero and yet our goal to connect with customers remained just as strong?
How could we achieve this in practice and how can we leverage data-driven strategies to make it happen? This is exactly what this project explores.
